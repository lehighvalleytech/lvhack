About The LVHack 2015 Weekend

Pitch It, Hack It, Show It

Anyone is welcome to pitch their project idea and receive feedback from their peers. Teams organically form around the top ideas and then it’s a 54 hour frenzy of designing, coding, and hacking. The weekend culminates with presentations and prizes!

Like Hacking in the City?
You'll love #LVHack

A big thank you to Velocity for hosting us this year. Velocity is an urban coworking space at City Center. It's a great venue, only a couple of blocks away from the new City Center in Allentown. Once the HackClock starts - you won't want to leave.

Fabulous Prizes and Great Food.

There are two things that are hackathons are famous for: Prizes and Food. We know that you have probably gone to other hackathons and came away empty handed and feeling like you ate pizza all weekend. That doesn't happen at our hackathons. We gaurantee you won't be disappointed.

About The Organizers

Lehigh Valley Tech is a local non-profit organization that promotes technology and entrepreneurship in the Lehigh Valley. Our events are for start-ups, entrepreneurs, hackers, and technology enthusiasts in the Lehigh Valley to share and demo what they are working on.
